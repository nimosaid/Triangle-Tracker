TRIANGLE TRACKER

A triangle is a plane figure with three straight sides and three angles.

TYPES OF TRIANGLES

There are three types of triangles:

  • Scalene
  • Equilateral
  • Iscoceles

A scalene is a triangle that has three unequal sides.An equilateral has all three sides equal.Lastly, an iscoceless is a triangle with two equal sides.

FORMULA

This are mathematical formulas asssociated with triangles:

  • Area of a triangle = 1/2 * base * height
  • Perimeter of a triangle = addition of all three sides.
